\subsubsubject{Joysticks}

\textDescrHead{Without front broom (or with front broom deactivated):}
The two joysticks each control one broom independently: Raise|/|lower~(\textSymb{joystick_aa}) or left|/|right~(\textSymb{joystick_gd}). The left joystick controls the left broom, the right joystick the right broom.\footnote{When the vehicle is fitted with a front broom (optional equipment), the front broom must be deactivated before the side brooms can be repositioned (\textSymb{joy_key_frontbrush_act} button).}

\textDescrHead{With front broom:}
The left joystick is used to raise|/|lower the front broom (\textSymb{joystick_aa}) and move it left|/|right (\textSymb{joystick_gd}). The right joystick is used to incline the broom forward and back~(\textSymb{joystick_aa}) and side to side~(\textSymb{joystick_gd}).
